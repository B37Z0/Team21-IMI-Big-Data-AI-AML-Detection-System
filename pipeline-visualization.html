Stage 03 — Three-Pillar Hybrid Ensemble
final_score = 0.50 × dynamic_score_norm
              + 0.20 × (1 − coverage) × if_score_weighted
              + 0.30 × adjusted_kmeans_score
Pillar 1
Weight: 50% — Corroborated Risk

IF × Rule Dynamic Score

Multiplicative gate requiring both the Isolation Forest and the rule engine to agree. Per typology: dynamic_i = if_score_i × rule_score_i. Sum across 5 typologies is min-max normalised across the full population. A customer who saturates rules but has a low IF score (or vice versa) cannot score highly — both independent models must fire together.

Pillar 2
Weight: 20% — Zero-Day Fallback

Coverage-Based IF Fallback

Safety net for sophisticated actors who evade all rule thresholds. coverage = fraction of 5 typologies where a rule fired. Fallback contribution scales with inverse coverage: (1 − coverage) × if_score_weighted. When all typologies have rule signal, Pillar 2 contributes nothing. When no rules fire at all, the IF score carries its full 20% weight.

Pillar 3
Weight: 30% — Behavioral Peer-Grouping

KMeans Within-Cluster Percentile

KMeans on the 5 raw IF scores (RobustScaled). k auto-selected by silhouette score over k ∈ [4, 12]; thin-cluster refit safeguard (≥ 200 members/cluster). Within each cluster, customers are ranked by if_score_mean percentile relative to their behavioral peers. Scaled by cluster risk tier [0.1, 1.0] — a floor that prevents the safest cluster from being a blanket exemption.
